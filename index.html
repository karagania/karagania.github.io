About Us

At Darajaat Integrated Schools, we are more than educators; we are cultivators of dreams and builders of character. Our passion lies in creating a nurturing environment where children don't just learn—they thrive, explore, and grow into well-rounded individuals. Guided by Islamic values and a commitment to excellence, we empower students to excel academically, emotionally, and spiritually.

Our Vision

We envision a generation of lifelong learners who lead with faith, wisdom, and compassion. Our mission is to inspire positive change in the global community by fostering academic excellence, ethical leadership, and a love for learning.

Darajaat's Unique Approach to Education

Little Minds, Big Dreams: Our Holistic Development Program

Our primary goal is to nurture every child’s intellectual, emotional, and spiritual growth. Through a carefully designed curriculum that blends the Cambridge Curriculum with strong ethical foundations, we ignite curiosity, creativity, and a passion for learning.

Step 2: Let’s Meet Your Star!

For students entering Grade 1 and above, we invite your child for a friendly and engaging interview. This is a wonderful opportunity for us to get to know your child and assess their readiness for the next step in their educational journey.
Note: Foundation classes do not require an interview – we’re ready to welcome your little one with open arms!

Once you’ve chosen your program and completed the interview (if applicable), it’s time to finalize the admission process. Simply pay the admission fee and submit the completed admission form to secure your child’s place at Darajaat..
